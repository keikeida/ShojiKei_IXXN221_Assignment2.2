About Us

The Beginning

Our Fairy Godmother began her studies at Hogwarts in her childhood. During her time at Hogwarts, she became deeply interested in potion studies and decided to begin her career. After her graduation from Hogwarts, Fairy Godmother takes an apprenticeship at Longslord's, the best potion makers in Too Far Away and gains the greatest knowledge and skills from the best. With nothing more too learn from the best, she begins her journey around the world and arrived to Far Far Away around about the time the king was entitled his throne. Beginning from the small cottage at the end of Far Far Away, we have now grown our buisness to the greatest potion makers of Far Far Away.

What is Our Goal?

Every person in their childhood has some sort of memory that has been marked into our skin for all our lives. We tend to dwell on things that stop us from our happiness. Our Fairy Godmother, noticed this during her time spent traveling and researching around the world. So to help us reach our happiness, Fairy Godmother began making potions that takeway our insecurities so we can live with confidence and pride in ourselves to win our own Happily every after. Our goal is to make every single person in the world happy, and spread love and peace to the rest of the world with our very own potions. Just one sip to your HAPPILY EVER AFTER.

How we make Our Products

All our products are cruelty free. Our main resources are fairy dusts that we stock in from faeries who stay in our glass jar hotels who kindly volunteer to donate their fairy dusts. Our organic Mermaid scales and tears are imported from Ursla (also a potion maker). Ursla is a company who has received top marks for her fairtrade and her environmentally friendly approaches made for her products. Our lovely swans we farm behind the cottage often accidently take out potions and show the results of out products that often turn out to be... fabulous of course.

Finally we bring our products to you with all out love and hopes for you in the future with your perfect life.

After all, we all need a

HAPPILY EVER AFTER.
